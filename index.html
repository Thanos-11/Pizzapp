ORIGINALE NEAPOLITAN PIZZA

Neapolitan is the original pizza. This delicious pie dates all the way back to 18th century in Naples, Italy.

pizza toppings are fresh mozzarella, tomatoes, basil leaves, oregano, and olive oil.



SICILIAN PIZZA

Also known as "sfincione is a thick cut of pizza with pillowy dough, a crunchy crust, and robust tomato sauce.

It is a square-cut pizza served with or without cheese, and often with the cheese underneath the sauce

The ingredients are often with bits of tomato, onion, anchovies, and herbs.



TIROLESE PIZZA

Originating from South Tiro in Italy Tirolese is based on ingredients that are characteristic of this region, a classic for many

Ingredients are tomato sauce, mozzarella, speck (smoked, cured ham) and sometimes mushrooms.
